Create a Renaissance mural painting that depicts a biblical scene. Jesus Christ should appear as the most important figure in the composition. Place him at the center of the table, where his calm and stable presence contrasts with the sudden movement around him. His head slightly bowed, looking down, is framed by natural light coming from the background, and the vanishing point of the room falls directly behind him, so the entire space guides the viewer’s attention toward him. Use calm, human colors throughout the scene, with the red and blue worn by Jesus Christ functioning as strong visual accents. The earthy tones and soft greys of the disciples should create a subtle contrast that keeps Jesus Christ at the center of attention and preserves the sober, tense atmosphere of the biblical moment. It is not heavily decorated, but it holds bread and wine, which symbolize the body and blood of Jesus Christ, powerful Christian symbols that deepen the meaning of the moment. On Jesus Christ’s left, include a group of three figures reacting to his words. One of them leans forward with his elbow on the table and has just knocked over a small container of salt, suggesting the moment right before the betrayal. Their gestures should feel tense and immediate, as if the announcement has just broken the stillness of the room. The unity of the entire creation is reinforced by the central image of Jesus Christ, whose presence visually and symbolically holds the whole scene together.
