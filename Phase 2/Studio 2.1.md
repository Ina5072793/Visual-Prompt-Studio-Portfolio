Assignment title: Visual promt studio 2.1.

Prompt A — Colored Pencil Drawing (Street Scene) 

Create a drawing that uses colored pencils to emphasize line, shape, contrast, and unity. Use a range of colored pencils, blending stumps, and erasers to create soft gradients and subtle textures, employing layering and cross-hatching techniques characteristic of this medium. The composition should feature a street with buildings in a row, showing perspective and depth, with windows, doors, and street details. The colors should convey a balance between warm and cool tones, with shadows and highlights creating a dynamic sense of light and atmosphere.

Prompt B — Linoleum Print (The same visual design) 

Create a linoleum print emphasizing line, shape, contrast, and texture of a street with buildings in a row. The linoleum print should use sharp cuts, bold outlines, and areas of solid black and white to define windows, doors, rooftops, and street details. Emphasize the strong contrast between light and shadow to convey depth, perspective, and atmosphere. The composition should capture a dynamic urban scene, highlighting the structure of the buildings and the rhythm of the street, while maintaining a visually striking and graphic quality suitable for printmaking.
